How It Works

Project Midas collects relevant tweets about the specified stock and runs a sentiment analysis to identify if public opinion on the company is postive or negative. It then makes a prediction for tomorrow's stock price by analyzing historical stock price data from the last 20 days and running them through the radial basis function (RBF) which is a type of support vector used in machine learning and neural nets.
